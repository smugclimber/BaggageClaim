YOUR VIRTUAL SUITCASE

A travel app that allows you to track all of your possessions from destination to destination. Input your items at destination A. Check them off when you arrive at destination B. Simple!




Services

Baggage Claim offers three item accounatability services.

Travel

a virtual suitcase for domestic and international travel as well as gym, overnight, and fashion styling jobs, etc. Keep track of all your goods as you move from point A to point B.

Relocation

A virtual UHaul for relocation. Keep track of your items as you move into that new apartment, on campus into a dorm, into a new office, or across the country into a new home.

Claim Support

Baggage Claim logs your items, each individual valuation, as well as the valuation of the entire suitcase. If property damage or theft occurs, Baggage Claim will submit your log directly to your insurance provider for additional claim support.
