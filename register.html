Baggage Claim

Services

Baggage Claim offers three item accountability services.

Travel

A virtual suitcase for domestic and international travel, as well as gym and overnight bags. Keeps track of all your goods as you move from point A to point B.

Relocation

A virtual UHaul for relocation. Keeps track of your items as you move into that new apartment, into a dorm, into a new office, or across the country into a new home.

Claim Support

Baggage Claim logs your items, each individual value, as well as the value of the entire suitcase. If property damage or theft occurs, Baggage Claim will submit your log directly to your insurance provider for additional claim support.
